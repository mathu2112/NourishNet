 **NourishNet**

NourishNet is an AI-powered food redistribution platform that helps reduce food waste by connecting restaurants with surplus food to suitable recipients such as NGOs, elderly homes, and community organizations.

The platform ensures that usable food is redirected efficiently instead of being wasted, creating both social and environmental impact.

 **Problem Statement**

Food waste is a major issue globally and in the UAE, where large amounts of safe, edible food are discarded daily from restaurants and businesses.

At the same time, many communities and individuals still face food insecurity.

**The main challenges include:**

Lack of a structured system to redistribute surplus food
Poor coordination between food providers and recipients
Delays in communication leading to food spoilage
Missed opportunities to convert waste into social impact
 **Solution**

NourishNet solves this by creating a smart digital platform that connects food providers directly with recipients.

Restaurants can log surplus food in real time
The system intelligently matches food with suitable NGOs based on urgency, type, and quantity
Donations are tracked until completion
Impact is measured through meals saved and contributions made

This ensures faster, smarter, and more efficient food redistribution.

 **Tech Stack**
Frontend: HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript
Backend: Python, Flask
Hosting: Hugging Face Spaces
Logic: Rule-based smart matching system (AI-inspired allocation logic)
Data: Demo / sample dataset for prototype simulation

 **Key Features**
 
 **Surplus Food Logging**

Restaurants can add food details such as type, quantity, and expiry time for redistribution.

 **Smart Matching System**

Automatically matches surplus food with the most suitable recipient based on urgency and capacity.

**Donation Tracking**

Provides real-time status updates from donation creation to successful allocation.

 **Impact Dashboard**

Displays total meals saved, environmental impact, and contribution statistics.

**Gamification**

Includes badges, donation streaks, and a leaderboard to encourage participation.

🌍 **Vision**

NourishNet aims to build a scalable ecosystem where surplus food is efficiently redistributed, reducing waste and supporting communities across the UAE through technology
